The following attributes are checked for broken links:

TagAttribute
<base>href
<link>href
<script>src
<blockquote>cite
<ins>cite
<del>cite
<a>href
<q>cite
<img>src
longdesc
<area>href
<input>src

Though they contain URLs, the following attributes are not checked:

TagAttribute
<codebase>any
<form>action
